## 1. Who we are

El D'Ora ("El D'Ora," "we," "us," or "our") is a maison of haute joaillerie operated by **Emporeko Global** in partnership with **Bestly LLC**. Our website is eldoraluxe.com. Our concierge contact is concierge@eldoraluxe.com.

The data controller for the purposes of GDPR is Emporeko Global, [REGISTERED ADDRESS]. For California-specific inquiries, see Section 9.

## 2. What information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to serve you. The categories below describe what we receive and how it reaches us.

**Information you give us:**
- Name, email address, and (optionally) phone number when you book a Private Appointment.
- Email address when you subscribe to The El D'Ora World newsletter.
- Design preferences, pillar choices, stone selections, and free-text descriptions you provide in the AI Design Studio.
- Billing and shipping addresses, government-identification (where legally required for high-value international shipments), and partial payment metadata when you purchase or commission a piece.
- Photographs or reference images you upload to the Bespoke flow.

**Information we receive automatically:**
- Device, browser, language, and approximate location derived from your IP address.
- Pages visited, time on page, items viewed, items added to wishlist, and referring URL.
- Cookie identifiers and similar technologies (see Section 6).

**Information from third parties:**
- Payment-status information from Stripe (we never see or store full card numbers — Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and handles cards directly).
- Diamond grading and certification records from IGI and GIA, which we associate with the piece you have commissioned or purchased.
- Shipping status and proof-of-delivery from our insured carriers (FedEx Custom Critical, Brink's, Malca-Amit, or equivalent).

We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please write to concierge@eldoraluxe.com and we will delete it.

## 4. Who we share it with

We share personal data only with the parties below, and only the minimum necessary:

- **Stripe, Inc.** — payment processing
- **IGI, GIA** — diamond certification (only stone details, never your personal data)
- **Insured shipping carriers** — name and delivery address only
- **Our atelier and workshop partners** (in Paris, Dubai, and our manufacturing partner in India) — to produce your piece
- **Email service provider** (Klaviyo or successor) — to send transactional and marketing email
- **Hosted platform providers** (Lovable, AWS, Cloudflare) — infrastructure
- **Professional advisors** (auditors, accountants, lawyers) — under strict confidentiality
- **Government authorities** — only when legally required (subpoenas, court orders, tax filings, AML disclosures)

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertising networks except as described in Section 6.

## 6. Cookies and tracking

We use the minimum set of cookies necessary to operate the site, plus opt-in analytics:

- **Strictly necessary cookies** — session, security, language. These cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics cookies** — Plausible Analytics (or equivalent privacy-respecting alternative). We do not use Google Analytics.
- **Marketing cookies** — set only if you have consented via our cookie banner. Includes Meta Pixel and Pinterest Tag when active.

You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner at any time. Disabling marketing cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

## 7. How long we keep your data

- **Newsletter subscribers:** until you unsubscribe, plus 30 days for archival.
- **Customer order records:** 7 years after the last transaction (tax retention requirement in most jurisdictions).
- **Bespoke commission archives:** for the life of the piece, so we can honor lifetime resizing and repair.
- **Marketing analytics:** 26 months.
- **Server logs:** 90 days.

If you ask us to delete your data, we will, except where retention is required by law (e.g., tax records).

## 8. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data, subject to legal retention obligations
- **Restrict or object** to certain processing
- **Receive** a portable copy of your data
- **Withdraw consent** for marketing at any time
- **Lodge a complaint** with your data protection authority (in the EEA: your national DPA; in California: the California Privacy Protection Agency)

To exercise any right, write to concierge@eldoraluxe.com. We respond within 30 days.

## 9. California (CCPA / CPRA) specific rights

If you are a California resident:

- You have the right to know what personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the third parties we have shared it with.
- You have the right to delete that personal information.
- You have the right to correct inaccurate personal information.
- You have the right to opt out of any sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. **El D'Ora does not sell personal information** in the conventional sense and only "shares" data with third parties for our own operational purposes.
- You have the right to limit the use of sensitive personal information.
- You have the right not to be retaliated against for exercising any of these rights.

To exercise these rights, write to concierge@eldoraluxe.com or call [PHONE — TBD]. We will verify your identity before responding.
