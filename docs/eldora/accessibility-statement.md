## Our commitment

El D'Ora is committed to making haute joaillerie accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We design, build, and maintain eldoraluxe.com in line with the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA**, the conformance standard most widely recognized internationally.

## Conformance status

This site is currently rated **partially conformant** with WCAG 2.1 Level AA, meaning some content may not yet fully meet the standard. We are actively working to remediate.

The site's accessibility was last audited on **[DATE]** by **[AUDITOR — TBD]**.

## Features we provide

- Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy, landmark roles, and form labels
- Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements, including the AI Design Studio wizard
- Visible focus indicators
- Sufficient color contrast (minimum 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text and UI components)
- Alt text on every product photograph and atelier image
- Captions on video content
- Resizable text up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
- Compatibility with current versions of NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack screen readers
- A skip-to-main-content link at the top of every page

## Known issues we are actively fixing

- Some product imagery in the archive may have brief alt-text rather than full descriptions
- The AI Design Studio's interactive stone preview is being enhanced with screen-reader-friendly text descriptions of each stage
- The site does not yet offer a high-contrast mode toggle (planned)

## Compatible assistive technologies

The site is designed to work with:
- **Screen readers:** NVDA (latest), JAWS 2024+, VoiceOver (macOS Sonoma+, iOS 17+), TalkBack (Android 13+)
- **Voice input:** Dragon NaturallySpeaking 16+, Voice Control on macOS and iOS
- **Browsers:** Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (current and prior major versions)

If you use an assistive technology not listed here and encounter difficulty, please let us know.

## Technical standards we follow

In addition to WCAG 2.1 AA, we reference:
- **EN 301 549** (European Standard for ICT accessibility)
- **Section 508** of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act
- **The Americans with Disabilities Act** (ADA Title III) as it applies to commercial websites

## Continuous improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment, not a one-time project. We audit the site at least annually, integrate accessibility checks into our development workflow, and train our team on accessible design. If you have suggestions, please share them.

## Enforcement procedure (for EU customers)

If you are an EU resident and believe we have not adequately responded to your accessibility concern, you may submit a complaint to your national accessibility supervisory authority under Directive (EU) 2016/2102 / Web Accessibility Directive.
