### 4.2 Bespoke commissions

A Bespoke Commission is a piece produced specifically for you, with your input on stone, metal, setting, and design. It begins with a Private Consultation and proceeds through our seven-step Bespoke Journey (described on /custom).

**Commission contract:**
- You pay a **50% deposit** to begin design and stone selection.
- The deposit becomes non-refundable once the CAD model is approved and the stone is procured (typically around step 4 of the journey, "Atelier Approval").
- The remaining 50% is due before final polish and certification (step 6).
- Lead time is **8–14 weeks** from deposit to delivery, depending on complexity and stone availability.

**Bespoke pieces are not returnable.** You may exchange for credit toward a different bespoke piece within 14 days of receipt, with a 20% restocking charge against the stone and labor costs already incurred. This policy reflects the irreversible nature of custom jewelry work.
